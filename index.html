Graphic Design

Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving through the use of typography,
and illustration. It is the art and practice of combining text and images to communicate
an effective message. It is also used to create visual content to communicate messages. Graphic design
is often used in branding, magazine design, advertising, packaging, and logo design. Graphic design is
used in many industries to create visually appealing products, services, and experiences.
